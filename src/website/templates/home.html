Search



Search allows users to look up necessities from supermarkets efficiently. It returns applicable results from most grocery stores within a 7 mile radius. Effectively then, each person minimizes the need to shop at multiple stores for their essentials — optimizing for social distancing and convenience. Although it only currently supports Irvine grocery stores that have websites, we expect to expand its functionality to most large supermarket chains in all areas.

Donate



Donate encourages all within the community to support our healthcare workers and the ill. With a shortage of ventilators and PPE for our healthcare workers, we strain our first line of defense and minimize the survival rates of those with COVID. Here, more than ever, we need to show our compassion for our community.
